FAQ

1. What is username in terms of login into the application?

--> Username is mothing but the employee id which will be provided after the registration of an employee for the first time by admin.

2. How to give attendance with this application?

--> You are supposed to scan the QR code given by company and you will be automatically logged in and same for the logout. While logout the last out-time will be considered.

3. Will I be able to give my attendance with different device other than own one?

--> No. As a security purpose while registration of an employee you will have to give your device's IMEI number which will be unique for your device of your device itself.

4. Will I be able to login/logout by scanning given QR code other than office premises?

--> No. It is strictly prohibited as another term of security where employee will have to do this activity within the office premises itself.

5. What if I forget to scan code for attendance for particular day?

--> You will have to fill manual attendance and get it approved with you manager.

6. How do I check whether my leave has been approved or not?

--> This particular information will be available in the 'View_leaves' tab under 'LMS' tab in the sidemenu.

7. Till what period the paysleep will be available for me?

--> The paysleep will be available of last 3 months only for every employee.
